Who am I?

I'm a passionate young lady with high enthusiasm, fuelled by Narrative and Media. I have a vision to bring the world together using the power of technology, eventually leading to world peace which will in turn enable us all to work together to speed up the development of life saving cures, global warming solutions and teleportation!

But one step at a time...

Short and Sweet

Growing up with a love of story books, I have always had a way with words and an over-active imagination. It wasn't long until I felt the urge to create my own narratives. My love of storytelling lead me to become Media Student of the Year 2013 in my Media L3 Diploma. I then taught myself to code, realising that Digital Media enables the audience to interact with my content, giving a whole new depth to the narrative that I can convey throughout my creations. From this I became a Front-End Developer in creative advertising agency, iris Worldwide. In the evenings my creativity knows no end, keeping me busy with my own creative outlet, my YouTube channel, YagmanX.
